Be careful: many “fund recovery”
offers are scams too

Legitimate legal support should not promise guaranteed results, pressure you into urgent payments, or ask you to send more money to “unlock” lost funds. Our approach is different: we review the evidence, explain realistic options, and help you understand what legal steps may be available.

Red flags to watch for:

  • They guarantee 100% recovery
  • They ask for crypto payments upfront
  • They claim to work secretly with government agencies
  • They pressure you to act immediately
  • They ask for remote access to your device
  • They tell you not to contact your bank, lawyer, or police
  • They ask you to pay “taxes” or “release fees” before funds are returned

The earlier you act, the more
options may be available

In online fraud cases, time can matter. Payment records, platform accounts, wallet addresses, communication history, and bank documentation may help determine what steps can be taken. A case review helps organize the facts before more evidence is lost.

  • Save transaction records
  • Avoid sending additional funds
  • Contact relevant financial institutions
  • Review possible civil, regulatory, or payment dispute options
  • Understand whether legal action may be practical
